Toyota Corolla 2024 Overview

The price of Toyota Corolla 2024 in Pakistan ranges from PKR 6,169,000 for the base variant Altis X Manual 1.6 to PKR 7,799,000 for the top of the line Altis Grande X CVT-i 1.8 Black Interior variant.

Toyota has done some minor changes in the 1.8 (2ZR- FAE) Engine to produce more power output from the same engine used in the previous engine it is also expected that Toyota will discontinue the XLi/GLi variant to introduce Toyota Vios in Pakistan.
